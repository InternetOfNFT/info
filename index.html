The project aims to enhance NFT functionality by utilizing advanced AI techniques to hide text within images, creating multi-layered steganographic NFTs. This technology enables the storage and transmission of hidden text, allowing for information transfer at different levels. The webpage provides practical experiences with steganographic applications and serves as a test interface for the Internet of NFTs. The applications of steganographic NFTs are diverse, including digital identity, tickets, and sharing. This technology has the potential to revolutionize industries such as art, gaming, and finance. The NFT Internet concept integrates data transfer, communication, and AI, enhancing traceability and value. It also facilitates advertising and NFT community engagement strategies. Ongoing research focuses on using NFTs as functional USB drives. The project has achieved significant milestones with a zero budget. Overall, it enables efficient data hiding within images, expanding NFT functionality and opening up innovative possibilities.

How to Work?

Multi-layer NFTs are a new form of NFTs where data is hidden within images. It is a steganography application that encodes the pixels of an image based on the ASCII equivalents of the text to be embedded. We have developed a program that allows us to hide text within images. This project is the first step of an initiative aimed at diversifying the usage of NFTs. Each artwork in this collection contains a hidden textual information. To access the content of your NFT, you can download the decoding program from the GitHub link provided. The images you are currently seeing are the world's first Stenographic Multi-layer NFTs. The proceeds generated from this collection will be used to further develop the Internet of NFTs protocol, which is an integral part of our future plans. For more detailed information, you can visit our Twitter account (@IONProtocols) or explore our program on GitHub.

In our high data load module, we follow a very different path. With a special algorithm we developed, we represent your data as 21 bits and match this representation with 3 7-bit ASCII symbols (letters). We connect the letter we matched to a pixel value of the picture. In this way, we can process 10 000 bytes of data into just one of your pixels so that it can be called. Our current work is to improve the data recall process with compression algorithms and turn it into an official USB memory stick and bring our Internet of NFT module together with this interface. ​

The actions you will perform in version 1.0 have a 250 character limit. The data that you will upload data to the picture is taken from the textbox and reprinted in the textbox in the analysis process. The hiding process takes place as follows; The data you write in the textbox is converted to ASCII numbers. Each letter is represented by a set of numbers and is matched to numerical expressions of the image's pixel values. While analyzing, this process is reversed and numbers are reached from pixel values and your text is reached from numbers. ​

Attention

Please note that this is not a data compression method. It is rather a way to convert your data into a format that can be decoded using two keys. If you want to embed your large-sized data into an image, you will need these keys for the decoding process.

INTERNET OF NFT (ION)

The concept of "Internet of NFTs" is developed based on the concept of "Internet of Things" and incorporates communication protocols with features such as multi-layered NFTs capable of carrying data, communicating, and partially thinking with the help of artificial intelligence modules. The Internet of NFTs focuses on the utilization of NFTs, which are unique and traceable representations of digital assets. This concept emphasizes the ability of NFTs to carry data across different layers. In other words, NFTs can store and transmit data within themselves. Furthermore, the concept of Internet of NFTs includes communication protocols equipped with artificial intelligence modules that possess communication and interaction capabilities. These protocols can facilitate information exchange and interaction through NFTs. The artificial intelligence modules can perform specific thinking or decision-making functions and facilitate communication between NFTs. The concept of Internet of NFTs enables more complex and interactive management of digital assets, enhancing their traceability, interaction, and value in the digital world. This concept represents the evolution of NFT technology and reflects the convergence of advancements in data, communication, and artificial intelligence fields.
