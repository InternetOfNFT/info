The collection was created based on an idea of combining motorcycles and dragons. It consists of multilayer images visualizing dragons living in a fictional universe, with the keywords being motorcycles and dragons. In this universe, there are a total of 7 types of dragons, each with their own powers and characteristics.


1 - Land Dragons: Land Dragons are fifth-class dragons with classic abilities of breathing fire and flying. They reside in deserted islands, rocky terrains, and archipelagos.




2 - Water Dragons: This species is closely related to the Land Dragons and inhabits regions near the sea. They share similar characteristics with the Land Dragons, except they lack the ability to breathe fire. Instead, they have the power to control water, swim, and navigate swiftly through currents.









3 - Divine Dragons: These dragons dwell in the skies and never land throughout their lives. They possess significantly higher power compared to the first two types. They are called divine due to the origin of their flames being radiation diffusion and their possession of nuclear energy. Divine Dragons leave a destructive impact when they attack and are rarely seen by humans. They have exceptional speed capabilities and are more docile compared to other types.






4 - Black Dragons: This type is the aggressive version of the divine dragons and is infused with negative energy. Although their origin stems from the Land Dragons, they possess the same power as the divine dragons. Encounter with a Black Dragon leaves no chance for other species to avoid a battle. Another distinction from the divine dragons is their possession of dark energy.






5 - Cyber Dragons: Cyber Dragons are an artificial species created by humans specifically designed to battle other dragons. While they excel against Land and Water Dragons, they have never encountered any divine or black dragons, thus the limits of their powers are unknown.






6 - Ghost Dragons: Ghost Dragons are black dragons caught between life and death. Their powers are unknown, but they are believed to surpass the previous types in terms of strength. They undergo transformations and are divided into sub-categories such as Ghost, Meta, Multi, and Dark. Dark Ghosts are the most powerful, possessing all the characteristics of both divine and black dragons. There are only three Dark Ghosts known to exist. The Alpha King Dragon, who rules over all dragons, belongs to this category. Lastly, there is a legendary dragon. Born from the evolution of offspring between divine and dark dragons, it gave rise to the Ghost species. Although information about this dragon's type and characteristics is scarce, it is considered the ancestor of the Dark Dragons.
